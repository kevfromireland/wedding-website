Coming from abroad?

If you're flying into Ireland for the wedding, then the best airport to fly into is Belfast International. It may be falling apart, but it's well connected with Cambridge and is very close to Belfast! EasyJet have direct flights from Stansted for around £100 return. If you don't like airports then there are ferry routes with the car from Liverpool and Stranraer.

Staying in the area

Unfortunately, there's limited space at the Larchfield to stay, but it's very conveniently located to both Lisburn and Belfast with each just a short taxi journey away. Lisburn is a slightly shorter journey, but Belfast gives you a lot more options for hotels catering for every budget.
